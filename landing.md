1. Add your clients — Import or manually add clients. One-click onboarding sends a welcome sequence automatically.
2. Schedule sessions — Drop appointments onto the calendar. Reminders go out — no separate tool needed.
3. Send invoices fast — Create a polished invoice in under 30 seconds. Track everything from one dashboard.
